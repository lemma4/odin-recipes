Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

This delicious cheese sandwich is one of the best dishes due to the simple ingridients that are needed and how easy it is to make! The gooey stretchy texture of the cheese once the sandwich is made is one of the most satisfying things to observe in this world.

Ingridients

  • White Bread
  • Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • White American Cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

Grab two slices of bread and apply butter to one side of each slice. Take a slice of cheddar and a slice of white american cheese and put them on one of the slices of the bread. Put the other slice of the bread on top of it. Place a skillet on an oven and wait until it is heated. Place the sandwich on the skillet and allow it to cook for 2 minutes. After two minutes, flip the sandwich and wait again. Prepare a plate, get the sandwich off the skillet and place it on the plate. Cut the sandwich and enjoy!
